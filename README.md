# AI玩转小恐龙实验室 (Dino AI Lab)

这是一个专为高中信息技术课堂设计的教学平台。平台支持教师和学生两种身份登录。

在这个“AI玩转小恐龙实验室”中，学生可以体验 3 种不同的 AI 方法来玩转经典的浏览器小恐龙游戏，从而直观地学习并理解人工智能的相关原理。

---

# Dino AI Lab

This is a teaching platform specifically designed for high school Information Technology classes. The platform supports both teacher and student roles.

In this "Dino AI Lab", students can experience 3 different AI methods to play the classic browser T-Rex game, helping them to intuitively learn and understand the underlying principles of Artificial Intelligence.
